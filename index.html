About WOD
WOD Notepad (World of Discoveries) is a focused note space crafted for ideas, plans, lyrics, code, and everything you don’t want to lose. Notes are stored in your WOD cloud using Firebase Realtime Database.
Built for mobile first. Designed to feel like a studio, a notebook, and a control room in your pocket.
